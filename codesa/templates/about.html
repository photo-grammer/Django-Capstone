CodeForChangeSA

History

CodeForChangeSA was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, united by their belief in the transformative power of programming and technology. The party emerged from a shared vision of leveraging innovation to address South Africa's pressing challenges and create a more inclusive and prosperous society.

Goals

  • Technological Empowerment: Our primary goal is to empower all South Africans with the skills, knowledge, and resources needed to thrive in the digital age.
  • Economic Transformation: We aim to harness the potential of technology to drive economic growth and entrepreneurship.
  • Social Equality: CodeForChangeSA is committed to building a society where every citizen has equal access to essential services, resources, and opportunities.
  • Transparent Governance: We strongly believe in the principles of transparency, accountability, and citizen participation in governance.

Principles

  • Innovation-Driven Solutions: We embrace innovation and technology as catalysts for positive change.
  • Collaboration and Partnerships: CodeForChangeSA values collaboration and recognizes that effective change requires collective effort.
  • Ethical Use of Technology: We prioritize ethical considerations in the development and use of technology.
  • Continuous Learning and Adaptation: CodeForChangeSA acknowledges that technology and societal needs are constantly evolving.

Join Us

Join us in our journey to use programming and technology as catalysts for change in South Africa. Together, we can build a future where innovation, equality, and transparency are the cornerstones of a prosperous society.
