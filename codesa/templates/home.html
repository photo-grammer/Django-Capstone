About Us

CodeForChangeSA is a political party dedicated to harnessing the power of programming for a brighter future in South Africa. We believe that technology and innovation can be powerful tools in transforming our nation and tackling the challenges we face.

At CodeForChangeSA, our mission is simple: to leverage programming and digital solutions to drive sustainable development, foster social equality, and empower all South Africans. We envision a society where technology bridges gaps, unlocks opportunities, and empowers individuals to shape their own destiny.
